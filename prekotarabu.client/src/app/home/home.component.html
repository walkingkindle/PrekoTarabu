Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to commonly asked questions about our chat application.

  • What is end-to-end encryption?

    End-to-end encryption ensures that only the sender and the intended recipient can read the messages. It prevents third parties, including service providers, from accessing the content of the messages.

  • Can I use the chat application on multiple devices?

    Yes, our chat application is designed to be accessible from multiple devices. You can use it on your smartphone, tablet, and desktop computer seamlessly.

  • Are group chats supported?

    Yes, you can create or join group chats with multiple participants. It's an excellent way to communicate and collaborate with friends, family, or colleagues.

  • Is my chat history stored securely?

    We prioritize the security and privacy of your chat history. All messages are encrypted and stored securely to ensure confidentiality.

  • Can I customize notifications?

    Yes, you can customize notifications based on your preferences. You can choose to receive notifications for new messages, mentions, or other activities within the app.
